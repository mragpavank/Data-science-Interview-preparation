Interview call/Getting selected  tips for jobs

1) Make a list of 75 companies which you wanted to apply.
2) Send out 100 LinkedIn personalized requests every week to recruiters working at those companies.
3) Connected with atleast 2-3 recruiters at every company and make initial conversations with them.
4) Reach out to them after preparation ends in 3 months.
5) When preparation was close to over, reach out to same recruiters already connected with the job profiles matching you from careers page for interviews.
6) Schedule 2-3 interviews every single day without wasting time.
